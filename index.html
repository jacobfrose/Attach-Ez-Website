Attach-EZ® offers a line of products that will enable framers to easily mount almost all types of Fabric & 3D artwork. Pat Kotnour, owner and creator of Attach-EZ Object Mounting Systems has been called the framers best friend by framers around the world. Her totally reversible methods and educational DVDs (which are included in the kits) now allow labor intensive projects to be completed in a fraction of the time, while still maintaining archival quality standards. Check out all our products, accessories and supplies in our ON-Line Store, or download our current catalog. You can also find video guides for using Attach-EZ® products here on our YouTube Channel as well as like and follow Attach-EZ® on Facebook.
